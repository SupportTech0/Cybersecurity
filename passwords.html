Passwords

Strong passwords are a critical part of securing your accounts. Tips:

  • Use passwords of at least 12 characters.
  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

Consider using a password manager to generate and store passwords securely. Check out these options:

  • LastPass
  • Dashlane
  • Bitwarden
